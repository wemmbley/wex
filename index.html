What is Wex?

Wex - small CSS library that take awesome things from Bootstrap, Tailwind and other modern frameworks!

Killerfeature - responsive and adaptive for mobiles, tablets, FHD, TV 2k, TV 5k+ from box!

Forget about UX-thinking! Just use -sm, -md, etc. tags everywhere!

font-sizes, spacing and colors FROM APPLE, - best designer ever.
